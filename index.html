About me

I'm a Software Engineer with experience in Scala and theoretical background in data science and machine learning with experience in Hadoop, Spark and Kafka.

In July 2017 I started working as Software Engineer at Gilt (HBC Digital) in the Personalisation Team.

Before that, I used to work as Data Scientist in the Data Science and Solutions team in AIB, one of Ireland's major retail banks with ~ 2.2 million customers. I improved the customer experience and created value for the bank by developing solutions able to provide real time insights. I developed solutions in Scala and Python, leveraging technologies such as Hadoop, Spark Streaming, Kafka, BigSQL, HBASE and Solr. One of the applications I developed with my team won the 2017 TDWI Best Practices Award for Data Management Strategies.

I graduated cum laude in Computer Science (with major in Business Informatics) at the University of Pisa with a thesis written during my internship at the corporate research centre of ABB in Germany. I did research on privacy preserving machine learning algorithms for the Industry 4.0. From my research activity, a paper has been presented to the IEEE International Conference and Workshops on Cloud Computing Technology and Science (CloudCom). The work has been published in the conference proceedings.
