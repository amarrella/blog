About me

I'm a Data Scientist with a strong theoretical background in machine learning and a Big Data Engineer with experience in Hadoop, Spark and Kafka.

In October 2016 I started working in the Data Science and Solutions team in AIB, one of Ireland's major retail banks with ~ 2.2 million customers. I improve the customer experience and create value for the bank by developing solutions able to provide real time insights. I develop solutions in Scala and Python, leveraging technologies such as Hadoop, Spark Streaming, Kafka, BigSQL, HBASE and Solr.

I graduated cum laude in Computer Science (with major in Business Informatics) at the University of Pisa with a thesis written during my internship at the corporate research centre of ABB in Germany. I did research on privacy preserving machine learning algorithms for the Industry 4.0. From my research activity, a paper has been presented to the IEEE International Conference and Workshops on Cloud Computing Technology and Science (CloudCom). The work has been published in the conference proceedings.
